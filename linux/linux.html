Why Linux?

Linux is a powerful and open-source operating system that provides freedom from costly licensing and frequent upgrades. By embracing Linux globally, both developing and developed nations can reduce technology costs while benefiting from a stable and secure system, supported by a global community of passionate developers and contributors.

Benefits of Using Linux

Cost-Free Accessibility

Linux eliminates the need for costly licenses, allowing all users to enjoy a free and fully functional OS with regular updates.

Security & Reliability

With an open-source foundation, Linux is constantly vetted by experts, ensuring high standards in security and stability.

Open Source Community Support

Driven by a dedicated community, Linux is continuously improved, adapting to modern needs while remaining accessible to all.

Our Approach to Accessible Linux Laptops

To bridge the technology gap, we are designing cost-effective, full-featured laptops for developing countries. These devices will run a robust, user-friendly Linux OS with a secure and simple update system. We are hoping to get the support from visionaries like Elon Musk and Larry Ellison, we aim to make quality technology affordable for all.
