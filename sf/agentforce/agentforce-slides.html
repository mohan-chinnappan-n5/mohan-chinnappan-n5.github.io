[![About](img/agentforce-1.webp)](img/agentforce-1.webp) ## What is an AI agent? - An AI agent is an intelligent system that can get work done **proactively and autonomously**, meaning it doesn't require human input. - Agents can be triggered by **changes in data and automations**. - They can understand and respond to human conversation **dynamically**. - They rely on machine learning and natural language processing (NLP) to reason and handle a wide range of tasks, from **answering simple questions to resolving complex issues**. ---
