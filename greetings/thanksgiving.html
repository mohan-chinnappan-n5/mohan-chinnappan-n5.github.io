About Thanksgiving

Thanksgiving traces back to 1621 when Pilgrims and the Wampanoag Native Americans shared a feast to celebrate the Pilgrims first successful harvest in Plymouth, Massachusetts. It symbolizes the coming together of different communities and gratitude for survival and prosperity.

Thanksgiving is a time to reflect on our blessings and express gratitude for the love, support, and joy in our lives. Families and friends gather to share a festive meal, traditionally featuring turkey (if possible, please pardon this bird!), and to celebrate the spirit of thankfulness and togetherness.
