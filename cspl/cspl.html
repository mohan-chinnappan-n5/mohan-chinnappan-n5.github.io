  • Login into your sandbox org or your PROD org and select Classic mode
  • Enter your Org Instance Url (Classic Mode)
  • Upload the input file (Pipe Separated) with format the following format by clicking [Choose File] 
                
            CountryCode|CountryName|ISO-Code|StateCode|IntegrationValue
            NO|Norway|NO-03|3|Oslo
  • Click [Generate] button to generate the recording script
  • Click [Save Source] to save the recording script to your computer
  • Click [Login] to land in your org
  • Load the downloaded script into Chrome DevTools Recorder and set timeout as 30000 or more
  • Click [Replay] in the Chrome DevTools Recorder
